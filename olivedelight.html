Where it all began...

Our founders, had an idea to create a restaurants that were more than a place that served great food. we wanted to give locals a place where they'd always feel at home. A place where they could get no-nonsense food at down-to-earth prices, and where they'd be treated right by people who had a passion to serve.The culture at Olive delight Restaurants was instilled long before a IndiAxis was ever written down on paper and is still evident today. The brands under Olive delight, guests, team members and community are all treated with respect.

Olive delight is a trademarked brand under Indi-Axis enterprises and is headquartered in Hyderabad, INDIA and is proud to serve more than 200,000 guests in its debut brand - Truffle Tower over the course of last year. We will continue to prosper and grow with two other brands launching in 2020.
